Privacy Policy


Routech collects some personal data from its users when they visit our website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Site. This data is solely for the purpose of interacting with our visitors users. Sometimes we also might use this data for the purpose of Analytics and understanding marketing trends. We do not use their personal information in any manner except as provided under this Privacy Policy.


Why is personal data collected?

The sole purpose of collecting personal data is to provide better services and results to our Users. We use this data to contact the user who has filled our contact us form hosted on our website. Users visit our website to contact us for services that Routech offers or they want to work with us. In both cases, the User authorizes this Website to use these details to call email them in order to answer their queries or contact them for open positions at Routech.
